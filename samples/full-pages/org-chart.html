Michael Ospitale | Interim CIO and Senior Director for DoIT Operations

Michael Ospitale was named Interim CIO on Sept. 5, 2015, after Cole W. Camplese resigned from Stony Brook University to accept the Associate Vice President and CIO position at the University of Chicago. Ospitale steps into the role of overseeing Stony Brook’s nearly 200-member central IT organization responsible for Data Network Services, Teaching, Learning & Technology, University Information Systems, Client Support, Systems and Operations, IT Security, and Research Technologies.

Ospitale moved into the position of Senior Director of Operations in the Division of Information Technology (DoIT) at Stony Brook in 2014. In this role, Ospitale was responsible for Endpoint Engineering, Client Support, Training, Vendor Management, and Shared Services. He led strategic sourcing efforts leading to significant savings while positioning the campus with important technology partners to assist with Information Technology Service Management (ITSM), Unified Communications and Shared Services. Prior to that, he served as DoIT's Assistant Director of Networking where he led the transformation of the campus network core, distributed antenna system for enhanced cell phone service, and the expansion of wireless throughout the campus.

Ospitale began his career at Stony Brook 17 years ago in the role of Assistant Director of Information Technology for Campus Residences where he built a full-service IT organization from the ground up. A graduate of Stony Brook University, he resides in South Setauket with his wife, Lisa, and daughters, Isabella and Brianna.
