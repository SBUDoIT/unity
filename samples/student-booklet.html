Manage Your NETID

SOLAR is your one stop shop to take care of all your administrative tasks here at Stony Brook. Take a moment to find out more about what you will be using SOLAR for during your time here at Stony Brook University.

SOLAR ID

  • Your SOLAR ID is an 8-digit number that will be provided to you during the orientation process.
  • Example SOLAR ID:12345678

SOLAR ID Password

  • Your initial SOLAR ID password is your birthday date in MMDDYY format.
  • For instance if your birthday was: July 25th, 1992, your password would be 072591.

NETID & PASSWORD

  • Your NetID is your key to online academic tools and services provided by Stony Brook University.
  • To view your NetID and change your password, login to SOLAR
  • Select NetID Maintenance under the Security and Personal Data section.

 WolfieNet

WolfieNet is Stony Brook University's wireless network on West Campus, Southampton, Manhattan, and parts of East Campus. It is available to all faculty, students, staff, and guests. New locations continue to be added as wireless technology and equipment continues to be upgraded and enhanced in order to handle the growing number of devices seeking to connect to the network.

Get-Connected

  • Use for the first time when connecting to WolfieNet. This option will configure Wi-Fi enabled devices to use WolfieNet.

Open

  • Available only in residence halls for gaming systems. Do not use for academic work or transactions such as banking or shopping.

Secured

Best Option

  • The recommended network that should be used on a day-to-day basis after successfully configuring Wi-Fi devices through WolfieNet-Get-Connected.

Guest

  • To be used by visitors to the University who do not have a NetID. Users must authenticate and agree to the Wireless Internet Policy.
