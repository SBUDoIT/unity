Introduction to Project Management Discipline and Tools

Training and Development is offering a 4-day course on DoIT's Project Management methodology, procedure, and tools. Some topics this class covers include: Planning a project to ensure successful delivery and stakeholder satisfaction, applying best practices to plan and run projects, implementing risk management, and an introduction to Project Portfolio Management tools (PieMatrix)

Workshop Level: Beginner Intended Audience: Faculty, Staff & Students

At The End Of This Workshop You Will Be Able To:


Describe Project Management Process


Apply the PMBOK Methodology to your own projects


Use SBU DoIT Project Management Office's tools in your projects


Establish a relationship with the Project Management Office (PMO)


Improve likelihood of project success through use of Project Management
