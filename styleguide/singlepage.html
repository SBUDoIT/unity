WELCOME TO STONY BROOK

Featured in this booklet are the most important IT services that you will utilize during your time at Stony Brook. Some of your instructors may ask you to access recorded lectures, or use online conferencing software, or clickers. You will quickly learn that each professor has a different way of incorporating technology into teaching, whether it be inside a traditional classroom or in an online learning environment.
We are confident that you will find a number of technology resourcesand services available to support your academic endeavors, and a knowledgeable information technology staff to assist you whenever you need help.

Your feedback is always welcome. Connect with us online using#sbudoit. We look forward to hearing from you about how we can improve our services.

Please let us know how we may assist you.

Cole W. Camplese
CIO and Vice President of Information Technology

Your NetID is an identifier used to access

  • WolfieNet Wi-Fi
  • Blackboard
  • Course Evaluations
  • Google Apps for Education
  • Library databases from off-campus
  • SINC Sites, COLAs
To change your NetID password
  • Log into SOLAR (stonybrook.edu/solar)
  • Under Security and Personal Data, select NetID Maintenance
  • Enter your new password
  • Wait 10 minutes for your NetID password to take effect
Passsword Tips
  • NetID passwords must be at least eight characters
  • Must contain at least one number (0-9) and one letter (A-Z)
  • Change your NetID password every six months
